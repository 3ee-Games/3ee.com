Code of Conduct

  • Published: December 13, 2021
  • Modified: April 13, 2023
    • Have a positive attitude and be kind to everyone. Remember that other folks see the world differently from you and assume you're in the wrong if someone calls you out.
    • Let people like what they like; don't hate on the preferences of others.
    • Profanity is to be used sparingly and as a tool for positive emphasis, not as a weapon to hurt people.
    • Don't skate the line of breaking the rules. When you're in doubt, you've broken the rules.
    • If you get called out for breaking the rules, thank the caller for the heads up, reflect, tighten up, and carry on.
    • No blatant advertising, spam, scams of any kind. If you want to share your creations with others, do it in a meaningful way.
    Consequences : Moderators can issue timeouts and other ways of curbing unwanted behavior in our games and community. If you receive a ban from any of our games, there is a chance you'll be banned from our Discord server and other games as well. Bans are not handed out lightly and we believe in giving second chances.

Reporting bad conduct

Report violations of 3ee Games’ Code of Conduct by contacting us. Do not feel ashamed for reporting other players that are harressing and/or threating you. We are committed to providing fun adventures for everyone.

Mistake or regret the past?

If you currently have limited account access and are ready to appeal your account restrictions, contact us. Everyone makes mistakes. 3ee Games is forgiving, even for repeat offenders. We all have bad days. Sometimes a string of bad days. All we ask is that you do not take advantage of our kindness and enjoy our games while treating everyone with respect.
